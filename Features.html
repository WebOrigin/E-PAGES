Panoramic Image

A great marketing tool for accommodation, restaurants, tourist spots, interior design, real estate, events, exhibitions and more you can think!
WebOrigin can provide panoramic photography for your products and services. It is an awesome interactive display function your customers won’t miss out. It is fun and easy to navigate the panoramic image by using the control bar. You can also just draw the image left, right, up, down by moving your mouse. Want to see more details of the image?
